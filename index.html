CARS CATEGORIES

Luxury

Our CarGo luxury cars are high-end vehicles that offer premium features and exceptional performance. They are typically characterized by their advanced engineering, elegant design, luxurious materials, and cutting-edge technology. Luxury cars often feature high-performance engines, advanced safety features, and advanced infotainment systems. They are also known for their exceptional ride quality, smooth handling, and superior comfort. Some of the most popular luxury car brands include BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Lexus, Jaguar, and Porsche.

Business

Our CarGo Business cars are excellent for business purposes, such as transportation to meetings, client visits, and other business-related activities. These cars are typically chosen for their reliability, comfort, and fuel efficiency. Business cars can range from compact sedans to luxury vehicles, depending on the needs and preferences of the company and the employee. Some popular business car brands include BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Lexus, Toyota, and Honda. Business cars may also be equipped with advanced safety features, such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and collision mitigation systems, to ensure the safety of the driver and passengers.

Pick-up

Our CarGo Pick-up cars have an open cargo area at the back, designed to carry and transport cargo or equipment. They typically have a cab for the driver and passengers, and a bed at the back for hauling items. Pickup trucks are often characterized by their high ground clearance, powerful engines, and rugged design, making them suitable for off-road driving and hauling heavy loads. They come in different sizes, ranging from compact pickups to full-size trucks, and can be equipped with various features and options, such as four-wheel drive, towing packages, and advanced safety systems.

Bride

Our CarGo Bridal cars are best vehicles to be use to transport the bride, groom, and wedding party to and from the wedding ceremony and reception. They are typically luxurious and elegant vehicles, chosen to match the style and theme of the wedding. Bridal cars can range from vintage cars to modern limousines, and can be decorated with flowers, ribbons, or other wedding decorations. Some popular bridal car choices include classic cars such as Rolls-Royce or vintage cars such as Volkswagen Beetle or Ford Mustang. Bridal cars are often rented from a car rental company or a private owner, and can be chauffeured or self-driven. They are an essential element of a wedding celebration, providing a luxurious and memorable transportation experience for the newlyweds and their guests.

Travel

Our CarGo Travel Cars are vehicles designed for long-distance travel and temporary living accommodations. They are typically larger than standard cars and are equipped with amenities such as sleeping quarters, bathrooms, kitchens, and entertainment systems. Travel cars can range in size from small camper vans to large Class A motorhomes, and can be self-contained or require external hookups for water, electricity, and waste disposal. They are popular among travelers who want the freedom to explore different destinations while having the comforts of home. Some popular travel car brands include Winnebago, Airstream, Roadtrek, and Forest River. Travel cars offer a unique way to travel and experience the outdoors, allowing for flexibility and convenience on the road.

Casual

Our CarGo Casual cars are best to use for everyday activities, such as commuting, running errands, and leisure activities. They are typically practical and comfortable, and designed for everyday use. Casual cars can range from compact cars to mid-size and full-size sedans, as well as SUVs and crossovers, depending on the needs and preferences of the driver. Some popular casual car brands include Toyota, Honda, Ford, and Chevrolet. Casual cars are often chosen for their fuel efficiency, reliability, and affordability, and may also be equipped with features such as Bluetooth connectivity, infotainment systems, and advanced safety features. They are a common sight on the roads, and are used by people of all ages and backgrounds for their daily transportation needs.
